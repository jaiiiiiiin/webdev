VISION



To improve man as man by developing individuals through a
legacy of excellent education and compassionate value formation.

MISSION



The noble dictum "improving man as man" embodies the existence
of the Our Lady of Fatima University as an educational institution dedicated to the
holistic formation of men and women imbued with the necessary skills, virtues, and
academic development.
