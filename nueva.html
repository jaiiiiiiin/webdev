Our Lady of Fatima UniversityNueva Ecija Campus

NUEVA ECIJA CAMPUS

Established: November 9, 2015

In pursuit of actualizing the bid to further spread its reach up to Northern Luzon, OLFU began to operate in NUEVa Ecija when they acquired and the Nueva Ecija Doctor's Colleges, Inc. (NEDCI) in year 2015. Located in Maharlika Highway, Cabanatunan City, Nueva Ecija, the branch now serves as Fatima's fifth campus

OLFU Nueva Ecija crowns "Ginoo at Binibining Wika 2018"

Date: September 07, 2018

In the recent celebration of "Buwan ng Wika" last August 2018, Our Lady of Fatima University-Nueva Ecija Campus held one of its biggest event to commemorate the observance through the "Ginoo at Binibing Wika 2018", a pageant that focused on empowering the Filipino language and its usage in the OLFU Community.
